ABOUT US

"Lost something? We're here to help!"

LostAndFoundDeport provides users with a unique online resource for the purpose of reuniting lost property with their respective owners (the "Service"). Using communication tools, personalized content,Student Product Recovery and LostAndFoundDeport provides a forum in which individuals can search or input lost and found advertisements to facilitate contact with other individuals in possession or with information regarding a lost or found object. Enter lost or found item details (e.g., item lost or found, category, photos, date, time, etc).


STEPS TO FOLLOW



1.Review details entered and submit the lost or found item report.

2.Receive an email with your user dashboard login and account information.

3.Within the dashboard you can print fliers of the submitted lost or found item.

4.Receive notifications when newly submitted items match the details on your entry.
